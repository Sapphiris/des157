Welcome to the world of Paküman!

We're all familiar with the creatures, "Pokemon", but what we're about to deal with are called "Paküman". You see, we live in an alternate universe, separate from the Pokemon universe. Based on Darwin's theory of evolution, creatures develop and mutate through natural selection in order to survive. Paküman are actually Pokemon, but with deformities that inhibit their survival. Scientists have devised a way to send these Paküman into our world, for them to prove their worthiness. Once worthy, they will be allowed to go back to the Pokemon world.


Your mission is to help prove which Paküman is worthy!
